Our Research Focus

The Department conducts cutting-edge research across a broad spectrum of mechanical engineering disciplines, addressing real-world challenges in energy, manufacturing, infrastructure, and health.

Our research programmes are driven by a commitment to excellence, innovation, and national relevance. Academic staff and postgraduate students work in well-equipped laboratories, collaborating with industry partners and international universities to produce high-impact research outcomes.

🔋

Renewable Energy & Thermal Systems

Research into solar thermal, wind, biomass, and hybrid energy systems. Covers energy storage, HVAC design, combustion engineering, and thermodynamic optimisation for sustainable infrastructure.

Solar PVWind EnergyBiomassHVACCombustion

Lead Researchers: Prof. Gunawardena, Dr. Fernando

🏭

Advanced Manufacturing & Industry 4.0

Exploring smart manufacturing systems, additive manufacturing, CNC optimisation, lean production, quality engineering, and the integration of IoT and AI into manufacturing processes.

Additive MfgCNCIoTLeanQuality

Lead Researchers: Prof. Jayasinghe, Ms. Abeysekara

Computational Fluid Dynamics & Heat Transfer

Numerical simulation of fluid flow and heat transfer phenomena. Applications in turbomachinery, aerodynamics, microfluidics, and thermal management of electronic systems.

CFDTurbulenceAerodynamicsMicrofluidics

Lead Researchers: Prof. Ratnayake

🤖

Robotics, Automation & Control

Development of autonomous systems, soft robots, human-robot collaboration interfaces, and intelligent control algorithms for industrial and medical applications.

Autonomous SystemsSoft RoboticsPLCHRI

Lead Researchers: Dr. Perera

Materials Science & Structural Mechanics

Investigation of composite materials, nanomaterials, failure analysis, fracture mechanics, and finite element modelling for structural and mechanical component design.

CompositesNanomaterialsFEAFracture

Applying engineering principles to biological systems — covering prosthetics design, rehabilitation engineering, medical device development, and human motion analysis.

Active & Recent Projects

● Active

Hybrid Solar-Wind Energy Storage for Rural Electrification in Sri Lanka

PI: Prof. A. GunawardenaFunder: National Science FoundationDuration: 2023 – 2026

This project investigates the design, optimisation, and techno-economic feasibility of hybrid solar-wind energy systems with battery and flywheel storage for off-grid rural communities in Sri Lanka's Northern and Eastern provinces. The project involves field installations, real-time monitoring, and simulation-based optimisation.

Renewable EnergySolarWindEnergy StorageNSF Funded
● Active

AI-Augmented Fault Detection in CNC Machining Processes

PI: Prof. N. JayasingheFunder: HETC / Industry PartnerDuration: 2024 – 2026

Developing machine learning algorithms for real-time fault detection and predictive maintenance in CNC machining operations. The project integrates vibration sensors, acoustic emission sensors, and deep learning models to reduce downtime and improve product quality in Sri Lankan manufacturing SMEs.

AI / MLCNCPredictive MaintenanceIndustry 4.0
● Active

Turbulent Flow in Micro-Channels for Electronics Cooling Applications

PI: Prof. P. RatnayakeFunder: NSF / DLR CollaborationDuration: 2023 – 2025

Numerical and experimental investigation of turbulent convective heat transfer in micro-channel heat sinks for high-performance electronics cooling. Results are being validated against experimental data obtained at the Department's Fluid Mechanics Laboratory and DLR facilities in Germany.

CFDHeat TransferMicrofluidicsDLR Collaboration
● Active

Low-Cost Prosthetic Hand with Myoelectric Control for Amputees

PI: Mr. K. BandaraFunder: AHEAD / Ministry of HealthDuration: 2024 – 2027

Design and development of an affordable myoelectric prosthetic hand for upper-limb amputees, leveraging 3D printing and lightweight composite materials. The project is conducted in collaboration with the Teaching Hospital, Peradeniya, and targets a clinical trial phase in 2026.

ProstheticsBiomechanics3D PrintingMedical Devices
◆ Upcoming

Green Hydrogen Production via Solar-Powered Electrolysis

PI: Dr. S. FernandoFunder: ADB Grant (Pending)Duration: 2025 – 2028

A proposed project to investigate the scalability and efficiency of solar-powered alkaline electrolysis for green hydrogen production, targeting industrial heating and transport fuel applications in Sri Lanka.

Green HydrogenSolarElectrolysisSustainability
✓ Completed

Characterisation of Natural Fibre-Reinforced Polymer Composites for Structural Applications

PI: Prof. K. WickramasingheFunder: NSFDuration: 2021 – 2023

Comprehensive mechanical and thermal characterisation of coir, jute, and banana fibre-reinforced polymer composites. Results published in 4 international journals and contributed to two MSc theses.

CompositesNatural FibresMaterials Testing

Research Laboratories

The Department operates 12 specialist research and teaching laboratories, equipped with modern instruments and testing facilities to support world-class research and hands-on engineering education.

🌡️

Thermal Engineering & Energy Laboratory

Head: Dr. Samanthi Fernando

Supports research and teaching in thermodynamics, heat transfer, HVAC systems, and renewable energy. Houses a solar simulator, heat exchanger test rigs, and a small-scale wind turbine test bench.

Key EquipmentSolar Simulator · Heat Exchanger Rigs · Calorimeters · Data Acquisition Systems · Wind Turbine Test Bench

🌊

Fluid Mechanics Laboratory

Head: Prof. Priya Ratnayake

Dedicated to experimental and computational fluid dynamics research. Features subsonic wind tunnels, flow visualisation equipment, and an advanced PIV (Particle Image Velocimetry) system.

Key EquipmentSubsonic Wind Tunnel · PIV System · Open-Channel Flow Rig · Centrifugal Pump Test Set · CFD Workstations

🏭

Manufacturing Systems Laboratory

Head: Prof. Nuwan Jayasinghe

Equipped for research in advanced manufacturing including CNC machining, additive manufacturing, and quality systems. Features an FDM 3D printer, SLA printer, and a 3-axis CNC milling machine.

Key Equipment3-Axis CNC Milling · FDM & SLA 3D Printers · CMM · Surface Roughness Tester · Acoustic Emission Sensor

🤖

Robotics & Automation Laboratory

Head: Dr. Rohan Perera

State-of-the-art facility for robotics research including a 6-DOF industrial robot arm, mobile robot platforms, and a real-time control hardware setup for rapid prototyping of control algorithms.

Key Equipment6-DOF Robot Arm · Mobile Robots · dSPACE Controller · Arduino & Raspberry Pi Platforms · Vision Systems

🧱

Materials Testing Laboratory

Head: Prof. Kumari Wickramasinghe

Comprehensive facilities for mechanical and physical testing of engineering materials including metals, polymers, ceramics, and composites. Supports both research and routine undergraduate testing.

Key EquipmentUniversal Testing Machine (100kN) · Charpy Impact Tester · Hardness Tester · SEM Access · Tribometer

🖥️

Computational Engineering Laboratory

Head: Dr. Chaminda Dissanayake

High-performance computing facility supporting FEA, CFD, and multibody dynamics simulation. Equipped with workstations running ANSYS, ABAQUS, SolidWorks, MATLAB, and OpenFOAM.

Key Equipment30 High-Performance Workstations · ANSYS · ABAQUS · MATLAB / Simulink · OpenFOAM · SolidWorks

🦾

Biomechanics & Medical Engineering Lab

Head: Mr. Kasun Bandara

Research facility for biomechanical analysis, prosthetics development, and medical device testing. Includes a motion capture system and access to a clinical trial space at Teaching Hospital, Peradeniya.

Key EquipmentMotion Capture System · Force Plates · EMG Sensors · Prosthetics Test Rig · 3D Bio-printing Access

⚙️

Main Engineering Workshop

Head: Mr. Wasantha Silva (Chief Technician)

Central fabrication workshop supporting all departments. Provides machining, welding, and fabrication services for research projects and undergraduate practical sessions throughout the year.

Key EquipmentLathe Machines · Milling Machines · Drill Press · MIG/TIG Welders · Sheet Metal Tools · Grinding Machines
