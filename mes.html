Mechanical Engineering Society

The Mechanical Engineering Society (MES) is the official student society of the Department of Mechanical Engineering. Founded by students, for students, MES creates a vibrant community that enriches life at the Department beyond the lecture hall.

From technical workshops and industrial visits to sports events, cultural activities, and community service, MES is at the heart of everything that makes student life at ME memorable.

Join MES →

What We Do

🔧

Technical Workshops

Hands-on workshops on CAD software, 3D printing, Arduino, welding, and other practical engineering skills not covered in the formal curriculum.

🏭

Industrial Visits

Organised visits to leading manufacturing plants, power stations, and engineering firms, connecting students with real-world industry practice.

🏆

Competitions

Organising and participating in national and international engineering competitions — robotics, design challenges, and hackathons.

⚽

Sports & Recreation

Annual sports days, inter-batch tournaments, and friendly matches fostering teamwork, sportsmanship, and a healthy work-life balance.

🎭

Cultural Events

The annual ME Night cultural show, fresher's welcome, and graduation celebrations that create lasting memories for students and staff.

🌱

Community Service

Social responsibility projects including school STEM outreach, environmental initiatives, and engineering support for underserved communities.
