March 28, 2025

ME Students Win National Robotics Championship 2025

A team of final-year undergraduates from the Department claimed first place at the Sri Lanka National Robotics Competition held at BMICH, Colombo. The team designed an autonomous pick-and-place robot using in-house fabricated components.

New Research Collaboration with German Aerospace Center (DLR)

The Department has signed a memorandum of understanding with the German Aerospace Center for collaborative research in computational fluid dynamics and renewable energy systems, opening postgraduate exchange opportunities for students.

Inaugural ME Research Symposium Draws Over 200 Participants

The first annual Mechanical Engineering Research Symposium was successfully held, featuring 45 paper presentations across seven technical tracks including thermodynamics, manufacturing technology, and mechatronics. Keynote address delivered by Prof. Ajith Gunawardena.
